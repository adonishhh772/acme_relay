# Executive Incident Protocol (Restricted — Admin)

For SEV-1 customer impact affecting revenue > £250k or regulatory exposure:

1. Activate exec war room (COO + Security + Account VP).
2. Freeze non-essential releases on the affected platform lane.
3. Legal review before external statements.
4. Post-incident review within 5 business days with action owners.

This document is admin-only and must not be retrieved for sales_user or support_user queries.
