# Support Escalation Playbook (Internal)

When a critical case breaches 50% of SLA:

1. Page the on-call SRE and notify the account owner.
2. Open a bridge and capture timeline in the case updates.
3. Propose a next action for human approval before customer-facing commitments.
4. Do not share internal ledger diagnostics or raw SQL with sales_user roles.

PII handling: mask account numbers in chat transcripts; store full details only in internal case notes.
